Advantages of BPA with respect to Branch of Engineering

  1. Reducing Waste-By automating everyday forms and workflows so they’re digital, college reduce paper waste as well as help the environment.
  2. Saving Time-There are administrative forms for finances and so on. Some secretary’s tasks can automated and paperwork time can be saved.
  3. Attendance management-Manage the attendance, send automated notifications and reports to parent and students.
  4. Course Registration-With automation, check student’s eligibility criteria, validate information, shortlist candidates to avoid long process manually.
